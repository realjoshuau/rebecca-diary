last entry.

the air tastes like matches. danvers has been preparing thingsi didn’t ask her to, but she knows.

maxim looked through me this morning. like i wasn’t there. good. i wore the black velvet. the one he hates. he flinchedwhich was the whole point.

i told him. not the truth—my truth. i said something that would make sure he remembered this night forever"i’m going to have a child, and it isn’t yours.".

he turned white. i saw his hands shake. he walked out like he couldn’t breathe.

good.

tonight, i’ll go to the boat shed. i’ve locked the cabinso no one finds what i’ve hidden there until it’s too late.

the sea is rising again.

i’m not afraid.

this is my house. it always will be.

no one leaves manderley
