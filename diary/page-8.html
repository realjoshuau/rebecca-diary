entry 8 — july 21



the doctor said it. i didn’t flinch. why would i? deathfinally, a guest worth inviting and i have been flirting for years.

i stood at the edge of the terrace. the wind kissed my throat like a blade.

i packed the last of the letters. danvers knows what to do.

jack begged me not to. he said he still loves me. i don’t think he knows what that means.

i will go to the sea. i will climb into the boat.



i will not scream.



i will not float.



i will go down laughing.



and i'll win.
