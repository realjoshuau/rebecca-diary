entry 4 — march 14



i saw the doctor. he called it fatigue. i smiled. he has no idea how many lives i’ve lived just this year.

i told maxim i’d be in london. he barely nodded. he's stopped pretending, finally. i watched him walk away. there’s a drag to his step now. good.

jack rang again. whining about god knows what. i told him to go to hell. he sounded like he already had.

in the mirror, my face looked… sharper. i touched the glass. it felt cold. not reflection. recognition.
